# Privacy Policy for QR Scanner Pro

**Last Updated:** February 13, 2026

## Introduction

QR Scanner Pro ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. QR Code Data
- **Scanned QR Codes**: When you scan QR codes, the decoded content is stored locally on your device for your scan history. This data never leaves your device.
- **Generated QR Codes**: When you generate QR codes for text or URLs, the content is processed locally on your device.

### 2. WiFi Network Information (With Your Consent)
When you use the WiFi QR code generation feature:
- **Data Collected**: Network name (SSID) and password
- **Third-Party Sharing**: This information is sent to our QR code generation service (api.qrserver.com) to create the QR code image
- **Consent Required**: You will be explicitly asked for consent before this data is transmitted
- **Purpose**: To generate a scannable WiFi QR code image
- **Data Retention by Third Party**: According to QR Server's documentation, submitted data is used only for image generation and is not stored permanently by the service
- **No Consent = No Transmission**: If you decline consent, your WiFi credentials will NOT be sent to any third party, and the QR code will not be generated

### 3. App Usage Statistics
- **Puzzle Streak Data**: Your puzzle-solving streak and statistics are stored locally on your device
- **Notification Preferences**: Your notification settings are stored locally on your device

### 4. Advertising Data
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. AdMob may collect:
- Device information (device type, operating system)
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android)
- IP address
- App usage data
- Ad interaction data

**How AdMob Uses This Data**: To show you relevant advertisements and measure ad performance. For complete details, see Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Ad Personalization**: We request non-personalized ads by default. You can control ad personalization through your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising; Android: Settings > Google > Ads).

## How We Use Your Information

- **Locally Stored Data**: Scan history, generated QR codes, puzzle statistics, and preferences are stored on your device for app functionality
- **WiFi QR Code Generation**: Sent to api.qrserver.com only with your explicit consent to generate QR code images
- **Advertising**: AdMob uses collected data to serve ads and improve ad relevance

## Data Sharing

### Third-Party Services:

1. **QR Server (api.qrserver.com)**
   - **What We Share**: WiFi network names (SSID) and passwords (only when you consent)
   - **Purpose**: QR code image generation
   - **Their Privacy Policy**: https://goqr.me/api/
   - **Data Protection**: QR Server processes data to generate images and does not store your WiFi credentials permanently

2. **Google AdMob**
   - **What They Collect**: Device info, advertising IDs, usage data
   - **Purpose**: Ad serving and measurement
   - **Their Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

## Data Retention and Deletion

- **Local Data**: All scan history and app preferences are stored on your device and can be deleted by:
  - Clearing app data in device settings
  - Uninstalling the app
  - Using the "Clear History" feature within the app

- **WiFi Credentials**: Processed by QR Server for image generation only. According to their service documentation, data is not permanently stored.

- **AdMob Data**: Managed by Google. You can reset your advertising ID in device settings.

## Reporting Inappropriate Ads

If you encounter an inappropriate advertisement:
1. Note the advertiser name or take a screenshot
2. Go to Profile > Settings > Report Ad in the app
3. Or email us at support@qrscannerpro.com with details

We will investigate and report inappropriate ads to Google AdMob immediately.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored locally on your device through the app
- **Delete**: Clear your scan history and app data at any time
- **Decline Data Sharing**: Refuse consent for WiFi QR code generation (no data will be sent to third parties)
- **Control Ad Settings**: Manage advertising preferences through your device settings
- **Opt Out of Notifications**: Disable daily reminder notifications in the app settings

## Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data:
- All local data is stored securely on your device
- WiFi credentials are only transmitted via HTTPS encryption when you consent
- We use secure third-party services (AdMob, QR Server) that maintain their own security measures
